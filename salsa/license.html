2. Product

The SALSA treebank version 2.0, consists of approximately 24,000 sentences of German newspaper text. The corpus was semantically annotated with semantic frames and roles in the Berkeley FrameNet paradigm in the SALSA (DFG) project, based on the syntactically annotated TIGER corpus provided by the TIGER project (Potsdam, Saarbrücken and Stuttgart).
